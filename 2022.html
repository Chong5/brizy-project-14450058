2022 Youtube Data Review Tutorial

1 - Export Data from YouTube

1.1 Ensure Language Settings

Please skip this part if your language is already set to English.

1.1.1 Go to https://myaccount.google.com/personal-info

1.1.2 Scroll Down & select General preferences for web - Language

1.1.3 Set Preferred Language to English in any region.

1.2 Google Takeout

1.2.1 Go to https://takeout.google.com/

1.2.2 Select Deselect all

1.2.3 Scroll to the bottom and check YouTube

1.2.4 Check All YouTube data included,

then deselect Videos

(or youll get a gigantic zip file!)

then click OK.

1.2.5 Click Next Step.

1.2.6 If you only need these data once,

scroll down and click Create export.

If you see yourself checking on these data in the future,

you can click Export every 2 months for 1 years

or any other available option.

1.2.7 Google will now generate your takeout

and will send it to your email once its done.

1.2.8 Check your email and download the zip file.

Now you finished all the hard parts.

Just the last two steps.

2 - Generate Report

2.1 Preparations

2.1.1 Go to app.branqi.com/2022_review

2.1.2 Upload the zip file you just downloaded from Google Takeout.

2.2 Your YouTube Rewind

2.2.1 Your report will soon be ready.

Note : The bigger your file the longer it takes, just refresh once a while.

Check out all the footprints youve left throughout this year!
