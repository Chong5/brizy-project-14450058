Overview

What is Branche?

Branche is building a Decentralized Digital Media Layer 0 Protocol, aiming to democratize digital media paradigm ;


Branche is a Web 2.5 content protocol aiming to resolve the difference in value orientations between platforms and content creators, building a sustainable platform for creators, consumers, and sponsors.

Problem & Solutions

Why did we start Branche?

In "Inevitable", the author mentioned that "In the past 25 years, humans have developed more than 60 trillion web pages, and with the exponential expansion of social media, humans share 18 billion pages with hundreds of millions of photos on social media each day". We are drowning in this vast ocean of data and its almost impossible to locate the information we need. 


The monolithic algorithm floods online platforms with indistinguishable screen fodders and quality content is buried deeper and deeper. Sponsors are paying more and more bidding for a preferred position. It takes us longer and longer to discover our desired content. In the end, the platforms are the only ones winning. The only solution to this problem, the only cure to this chronic ailment is a content filter that works 100% to your preferences.

Theres more. Tech giants take hold of our information and use it to get a tighter and tighter grasp on us. Branche wishes to cut this proverbial Gordian Knot, creating a new protocol to assert our sovereignty over our own information, giving data back to its owners, and taking back control from the algorithm.

Who is branche for?

Here at Branche, creators can build, grow and communicate with their community directly, cultivating your own ecosystem and bond with your subscribers. It will be your retreat from the omni-present algorithm, where our diversified recommendation system creates an equal opportunity for every piece of content. You dont have to put on a mouth-wide-open face or a red arrow to or gather traction or enjoy creating content. Branche also offers a more colourful collection of means of monetisation where you get to take the wheel instead of fumbling at the algorithm and working all day under it.


             Sponsors can enjoy a fresher breeze of air in this convoluted content economy. Content has become an indispensable link in every companys marketing strategy. Branche aims to better make clear and standarize content creation, integrating content and business into one efficient system.


             Users (content consumers) can now wield their bespoke content filter and have power over what they see. With a flick of a switch, you can flip your frontpage from a dashboard for researchers to an entertainment hub for couch potatoes. We offer decentralized identification where your data is yours and only yours. These data will then become you, an NFT that represents who you are.

Why we named it branche / blanche?

Branche speaks for our wish to branch out from this content ecosystem ruled by the algorithm and create a new, decentralized ecosystem for content creators and sponsors both, an ecosystem where every direction is forward. Branche is our solution for content creators. We encourage them to branch out into our open, inclusive ecosystem, away from the algorithm and even create content that may have no traction at all on traditional platforms. Branche is also our solution for sponsors. We aim to drive away the clouds in the content creation industry, creating a clear sky under which sponsors and content creators can cooperate and thrive.

Creators

Brief

We wish to offer every creator a retreat where they can be free from the algorithm and work for themselves and their subscribers. You create what you like, for those who like you, nothing more.

Creators will never have to work for any centralized entity as they did before. Their revenue can be generated not only from ads, but also from their community in a variety of forms. Our platform gives them full trust and autonomy on their community.

Community Utilities

Community

Building your community at Branche can be as easy as it is powerful. You can communicate and bond with your subscribers any time you want.

Community 101

Feedback:

Ask your subscribers what they think about your content and what they want from future content.

Co-creation:

Ask your subscribers what they want to see in your next video.

                

Interactions in Both Directions

Share your bloopers. Give your subscribers some homework and leave your feedbacks.


POAP

Customize your rules and give out POAPs to your subscribers.


You can also:

Host a tournament between your community and other creators. Generate content with these events and further expand your influence.

Start a fundraiser and kickstart your latest masterpiece with the support from your subscribers.

Take sponsors that are more focused on your niche and better serves your subscribers. Make sponsor segues more you, and more your subscribers.

Create highly personalized content for some of your most die-hard fans.

and more! With direct, personal communications with your subscribers, content creation and revenue generation will be made easier and more enjoyable.

Memberships

Create multiple tiers for your subscribers. Higher tier subscribers may enjoy more personalized content and experience.

Subscribers are also paying for themselves. They subscribe to you because they enjoy the content youve made and wish to support you with a long-term commitment. This commitment works both ways, of course, as you have to continue creating those contents to keep more subscribers. This will not translate to catering to your subscribers whatever the cost, however, as youre still able to make your unique content in an unapologetically you way. 

Tips & Donations

Branche will never take a dime out of your subscribers tips and donations. These tips and donations can be set as an entry pass for you community or even made into POAPs to keep records of your precious interactions.

Builders

Brief Overview for Builders

What is Branche?

Branche is a P2P platform for builders and creators. Builders can meet, negotiate, and collaborate with creators for years to come. We build booths where sponsors and creators display their orders. We also build transparent meeting rooms where they negotiate and finalize their deals. No one gets in between. Everything is open for all to see. No more sitting and waiting on your first sponsor. No more arbitrary cut to the platforms. No more shooting-in-the-dark ads that translates poorly into sales figures.

Sponsors

Sponsors may cut creators in as their distributers in a B2C2C business model, cutting red tapes once and for all.

Features & Utilities

Distributors

Creators and merchants are no longer hired and employed, but become a win-win relationship, promoting products to better match the users of content creators, so that the content and products are best matched. 

Sponsors can customize their revenue-sharing model and make everyone eligible as their distributor.


Limited-Time Events

Create limited-time offers or give out free samples to attract more distributors as well as consumers to your brand.

Pay Them Whichever Way You Like

Payments can be made in an ever-growing number of Web2 and Web3 channels.

Universal Product Validator

Validate and redeem product with our open-source, universal product validator.

Permissionless Licensing Protocol

Features & Utilities

Content Filter

With Branche, Consumers can filter digital content based on keywords, themes, and interests. You can overlay as many filters as needed until your recommendations are exactly what youre looking for.


Custom metrics

Consumers can set their own metrics for our algorithm to discover the quality content they truly need.


Knowledge map

With Branche, Consumers can divide contents into what they know, what they know they dont know, and what they dont know and tune the algorithm to recommend each when needed.

Taste model

Branche offers comprehensive control over your content filter. You can filter out any content at any given time with custom modes. Your productivity recommendations will have zero impact over your entertainment ones.

Value Props

You Personal Artificial Intelligence(AI)

Branche wishes to build a more personalized AI algorithm comparing to the general-purpose AIs that dominate the market these days. You get to tune your AI to be a better personal assistant, learn your preferences progressively, and transforms it into a personalized intelligence just for you.

            

Custom Algorithms

You can mix and match between different algorithms and even borrow from others. Your algorithms can also guess your preferences and even evolve over time. All those at your fingertips.

                

Follow Your Gurus

If you feel at loss and do not know where to start with, you can borrow algorithms and patterns from others, pick out preferences from their algorithms, or plan your AIs evolvement with their learning pathways.


DID

You take full control of all your information at Branche. These information will also be minted as your NFTs after your sovereignty over it has been asserted. These NFTs will later become your universal identity across multiple Web3 platforms. With all your interactions and achievements, it will also become your persona. They are essentially snapshots of your identity across different stages.


Informational Autonomy

Branche gives you full control over your personal information. You can choose the level of exposure for these information or even create unbacktracable aliases for different events. Your information will never be used against you.

              

You also get to choose to display your information in whichever way you deem necessary. For instance, you can show a selected set of information to help facilitate sponsored content.


Your Mind Palace

Our AI picks memories whenever and however you want for your reference.


An AI that knows you better

Branche is building a more personalized AI that serves you entirely, rather than the generic-purposed AIs we see these days. An AI that understands you and your knowledge spectrum. An Ai capable of teaching you in ways you best understand and even bringing back half-remembered memories.


AI as a medium

As our AI gradually gets a better grasp of your traits, it can grow to become a bridge between you and other generic-purpose AIs, give your commands to these AIs and revolutionalize AI assistants.

Extensive Features

Algorithmic Economy

Customized algorithms breed infinite algorithmic patterns. You can purchase or gift these algorithms as you would commodities. Information cocoons will be no more as people can freely choose between an infinite number of algorithms and watch/learn whatever they want. Cretors can also pack their own recommendation algorithms and sell it to their fans such that they may learn and grow as these Creators. The algorithm that best suits you can now be bought. Creators also get to profit on their personalized algorithms.
