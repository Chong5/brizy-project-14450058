Branche Protocol

Editor : North

ALL POSTS


"Branche is a Web 2.5 content protocol aiming to resolve the difference in value orientations between platforms and content creators, building a sustainable platform for creators, consumers, and sponsors. Branche wishes to create a new protocol to assert sovereignty over our own information, giving data back to its owners, and taking back control from the algorithm."

In "The Inevitable", Kevin Kelly mentioned that "In the past 25 years, humans have developed more than 60 trillion web pages, and with the exponential expansion of social media, humans share 18 billion pages with hundreds of millions of photos on social media each day". We are drowning in this vast ocean of data and its almost impossible to locate the information we need.

The Monoplistic Algorithm Dilemma

Creators also have to cater to the algorithm rather than create meaningful content. Monolithic contents flood our attention and quality contents are deeply submerged in this vast ocean filled by the algorithm. Branche wants to change this. We wish to offer every creator a retreat where they can be free from the algorithm and work for themselves and their supporters. You create what you like, for those who like you, nothing more.

Sponsors will also benefit from our protocol where they can meet, negotiate, and collaborate with creators for years to come. We build booths where sponsors and creators display their orders. We also build transparent meeting rooms where they negotiate and finalize their deals. No one gets in between. Everything is open for all to see.

Consumers gets full control over their data at Branche. We are your own content filter, your everyday information carry. Our algorithm serves you and you only. You can customize it however you want until you get your desired informational stream.

We are swarmed by disarrayed information streams every day. We dont get to choose what we see and cant help but get isolated in our little information cocoon. Branche proposes an alternative, where we take back control over our attention and emerge from the information torrent when everyone is amusing themselves to death.
