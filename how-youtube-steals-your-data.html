How YouTube Steals Your Data and Traps You With It

Do you know that the Web 2.0 giants became giants because of your data? They collect information about what videos you watch, what comments you make, and even what ads you click on. This data is then used to target ads and content to you, creating a "filter bubble" that keeps you trapped in a cycle of watching and clicking. YouTube also uses this data to track your activity and build a profile of you, which can be used to target you with even more ads and content. This can be a dangerous situation, as it leaves you vulnerable to exploitation and manipulation. To protect yourself, it's important to be aware of how YouTube collects and uses your data and to take steps to limit the amount of data it collects.

Branche wants everyone to know this. Our 2022 YouTube Review will give you a better look (from the outside!) at this cocoon that youre in. Simply download your data pack from YouTube and see what you have been watching in 2022 with a report from us.

Where do I see this report?

  1. Download your data takeout at https://takeout.google.com/settings/takeout (history may not be available depending on your settings).
  2. Upload your data package to Branche for an instant analysis of your total watch time, active days, word cloud, etc. You can go ahead and share this report on various social media platforms.
